Websites That I Think Are Cool


  • Based Cooking: Crowdsourced recipe website with no ads, no trackers and no bloat. Kinda ugly but better than having huge ads and autoplaying videos.

  • The New Oil: An excellent resource on why you should, and how you can protect yourself in the digital world.

  • OpenStreetMap: Private and open source alternative to Google Maps. (PLEASE contribute to it!)

  • Photopea: Decent image editing progressive web app. Don't edit any images that you wouldnt otherwise be fine with being public as it is cloud based.

  • Plexus: A crowdsourced database of Android app compatability status on de-Googled phones, both with MicroG and without.

  • Techlore: A general resource on all things privacy and security and home of the free "Go incognito" course, a 4 hour course on how to get private and secure digitally.
